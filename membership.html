Memberships

Our membership program provides families with full access to a warm, inspiring learning environment rich in community, creativity, and support. Members enjoy resources designed to uplift homeschool families and provide meaningful educational enrichment.

Included With Every Membership

  • Parent fitness groups
  • Media center for students
  • Full library with rare and classic titles
  • Instrument use and music access
  • Parent support groups & Mothers-as-Parents circles
  • Workshops, meetings, and community events
  • Full commercial teaching kitchen (by appointment)
  • Playground, outdoor lounge & fire pit
  • Gardening opportunities & outdoor activities
  • Community pantry access
  • Curriculum guidance and homeschool consulting
  • Teen & preteen drop-off for study/work support

Discounted Add-Ons

  • Parents Night Out
  • Cooking & baking classes
  • Paint & Pair (art + connection workshops)
  • Birthday parties & event hosting
  • Coffee bar access plans
  • Meal prep plan for busy families
  • Sewing, knitting, & quilting workshops
  • Woodworking & hands-on craft lessons
  • Seasonal programs & special events
