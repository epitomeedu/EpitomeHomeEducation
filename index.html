Enrollment Information

Thank you for choosing Epitome Education. Below you’ll find our full enrollment overview, application forms, requirements, and payment details. This page is designed so you can complete all steps easily — without scrolling the entire site.

Requirements

  • Completed Enrollment Application
  • Parent/Guardian ID
  • Immunization record or exemption
  • Emergency contact form
  • First week tuition payment

How to Enroll

  1. Complete the admission application.
  2. Email or bring documentation to the center.
  3. Attend a parent meeting (if required).
  4. Review and sign the Parent Handbook.
  5. Make registration + first week payment.
