About Epitome Education

Epitome Education provides a warm, structured, home-based learning environment where children can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. Our hybrid model supports working parents, homeschool families, and independent teens.

Programs

Hybrid Homeschool Program (Ages 3–10)

Structured curriculum, socialization, hands-on learning, and full-day care available from 6 AM to 7 PM.

Before & After School Care

Flexible care for homeschool or traditionally schooled students needing early drop-off or extended afternoon hours.

Parent Workspace Access

Homeschooling parents can utilize the center for lessons, projects, or quiet study spaces.

Teen Drop-Off Workspace

Teens ages 11–18 may use supervised workrooms for independent study, virtual school, or creative projects. All teens must follow the Teen Conduct Agreement for safety and professionalism.

Enrollment

We offer year-round enrollment with limited spaces. Families can apply for full-time, part-time, or teen-access programs.
