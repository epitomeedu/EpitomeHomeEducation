About Our Micro-School

Epitome Education is a thoughtfully designed micro-school focused on small class sizes, meaningful relationships, and individualized learning experiences. Here at Epitome Home Education, we strive to make the most enriching and thoughtful educational experience for both parents and children. We are very small so every child is treated with the utmost dignity and allowed as much nurturing and attention that he or she needs to ensure their success. As a mother, I want to provide the very best for my child and I have not been able to find that standard of care, which is exactly why I founded this school. Here, mothers will have the support and resources they need to provide their child with a fulfilling education and the peace of mind that their child‚Äôs wellbeing is the utmost priority. I will care for the children who attend this school as if they were my own without exception. Every bump will be nurtured, every meal will be home cooked, every lesson enriching, fulfilling and kind.
