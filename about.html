About Our Founder

I’m a single mom, entrepreneur, and educator at heart who believes deeply in the power of small, intentional learning communities. Originally from New York, my journey brought me to West Texas with one clear goal: to create a better, more balanced life for my child while building something meaningful for others.

After years as a chef and bakery owner, I learned firsthand the value of creativity, discipline, and learning by doing. As a military widow and an expat who has lived and worked across cultures, I’ve seen how essential stability, compassion, and strong support systems are—especially for children.

When rising costs and limited childcare made it clear that staying in New York was no longer sustainable, I chose to start fresh somewhere that allowed room to grow, breathe, and build community.

Epitome Education was born from that vision—a place where children are known, supported, and encouraged to learn with purpose and confidence.
